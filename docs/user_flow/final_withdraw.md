# Final Withdraw

## Overview

Complete guide to claiming your USDC after withdrawal request is ready.

## Claiming Process

### Prerequisites

✅ Withdrawal request submitted  
✅ Status: Ready to Claim  
✅ Have request ID  
✅ Wallet connected  
✅ Have MNT for gas

### Steps

1. Check withdrawal status in dashboard
2. Click "Claim Now" button
3. Confirm transaction in wallet
4. Receive USDC in your wallet

## After Withdrawal

Your withdrawn USDC options:

- Hold as stablecoin
- Re-deposit to SuperCluster
- Deploy in other DeFi protocols
- Off-ramp to fiat

## Verification

Check your wallet balance and verify on block explorer that you received the expected USDC amount.
